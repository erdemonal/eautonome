Description

This ontology provides the OWL representation of the Projet Maison Eautonome. The main objective of this research is to reflect on domestic water uses, associated minimum qualities, and possible optimization cycles, in order to reduce the water footprint of metropolitan cities. The Eautonome project aims to reduce the water needs of individual housing through the intelligent reuse of greywater.
